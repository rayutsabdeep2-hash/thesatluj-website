Our Story

My husband and myself are both from the corporate background and have always been passionate about travelling and have been to various corners of India. From the very onset we have been self driving everywhere we go. Knowing new places, interacting with new people, understanding their culture, learning their life style, trying various local foods have become a part of our travelling objectives and we gradually started enjoying every bit of it.

Travelling opened up new doors which prompted us to visit Himachal Pradesh for the very first time. Since then, we felt love in every bit of our experiences which ended in many more visits throughout Himachal Pradesh in the subsequent years but the place never seems to lose its beauty and holds a very special place at the bottom of our heart.

Our first and subsequent visits to Kalpa drove us insane with the beauty and serenity of the place and it was then when we decided to start with some venture of our own so that we can stay close to Himalayas and follow our passions of travelling more into the remotest corners of the villages as also, to enable travellers to stay with us and share their stories and experiences. This concluded in the creation and setting up of The Satluj for all people who are passionate about travelling and enjoy nature at its best.

— Priyanka & Family
